Storylines

Right-click a scene on the Timeline View to tag it with a storyline. You can then track how different storylines are advanced in the course of your script by toggling them using the drop-down on the left-hand side.

NOTE: This is a highly experimental feature for now.

Anti-Lateral Thoughts

By pressing the light bulb on your TouchBar, you can access a collection of thoughts inspired by Oblique Strategies. Useless and silly.
